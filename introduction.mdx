Smartcomply's developer documentation is concise and tailored to get you up and running in less than 15 minutes. We guide you through the essential tools needed to make your Smartcomply integration seamless.
<p>Smartcomply offers modern financial infrastructure for internet companies and developers across Africa, enabling access to financial data, issuing virtual bank accounts and payment debit cards, and collecting payments directly from bank accounts.
Our API offers robust capabilities to calculate and generate fraud probabilities for transactions and also monitor and report transactions, empowering you to detect and mitigate fraudulent activities effectively.</p>
<p>The Smartcomply API provides an interactive and easy-to-use structure that helps developers get started conveniently.
Smartcomply is currently available in Nigeria, Ghana, Kenya, Rwanda, and Uganda. However, the team is working to expand its services to other countries.</p>

<h3>Smartcomply Products:</h3>

<h4>KYC</h4>

<p>Smartcomply KYC enables you to know your customers and reduce fraud by validating their BVN, NIN, phone numbers, CAC, emails, IDs, OCR for facial recognition,
 and more. It utilizes various data points and verification techniques to easily identify and verify customers, 
 supported by a highly responsive AI system that captures customer biodata.</p>
<h4>Connect</h4>
<p>Connect to your customers and gain deeper insights by accessing their financial data, including full bank statements, income, real-time transactions, balances, and more. Connect provides secure access to high-quality, well-organized, and detailed user financial data.</p>

<h4>Direct Debit</h4>
Gain direct access to your customers' individual banks to receive and send payments with ease, without any rigorous processes. The Smartcomply Direct Debit API allows third parties to connect with other banks or financial institutions to facilitate easy payments. Direct Debit also provides a universal card that consolidates all your accounts into one card, simplifying transactions anywhere, anytime.

## KYC/AML

The AML/KYC (Anti-Money Laundering / Know Your Customer) API is designed to help organizations comply with regulatory requirements by automating the processes of identity verification, risk assessment, 
and ongoing monitoring of customers. The API integrates with various databases and services to perform checks against global watchlists, verify identities, assess risk levels, and ensure that transactions are compliant with financial regulations.

##Fraud and Transaction monitoring
The Fraud Transaction Monitoring API harnesses the power of advanced technologies such as machine learning and data analytics to provide accurate fraud detection solutions. 
By analyzing transaction patterns and user behaviors, our API calculates a fraud percentage, enabling you to make informed decisions and take proactive measures to safeguard your users and business.
