Smartcomply's developer documentation is concise and tailored to get you up and running in less than 15 minutes. We guide you through the essential tools needed to make your Smartcomply integration seamless.
Smartcomply offers modern financial infrastructure for internet companies and developers across Africa, enabling access to financial data, issuing virtual bank accounts and payment debit cards, and collecting payments directly from bank accounts.
The Smartcomply API provides an interactive and easy-to-use structure that helps developers get started conveniently.
Smartcomply is currently available in Nigeria, Ghana, Kenya, Rwanda, and Uganda. However, the team is working to expand its services to other countries.

<h3>Smartcomply Products:</h3>

<h4>KYC</h4>

<p>Smartcomply KYC enables you to know your customers and reduce fraud by validating their BVN, NIN, phone numbers, CAC, emails, IDs, OCR for facial recognition,
 and more. It utilizes various data points and verification techniques to easily identify and verify customers, 
 supported by a highly responsive AI system that captures customer biodata.</p>
<h4>Connect</h4>
<p>Connect to your customers and gain deeper insights by accessing their financial data, including full bank statements, income, real-time transactions, balances, and more. Connect provides secure access to high-quality, well-organized, and detailed user financial data.</p>

<h4>Direct Debit</h4>
Gain direct access to your customers' individual banks to receive and send payments with ease, without any rigorous processes. The Smartcomply Direct Debit API allows third parties to connect with other banks or financial institutions to facilitate easy payments. Direct Debit also provides a universal card that consolidates all your accounts into one card, simplifying transactions anywhere, anytime.

<h4> Why Smartcomply? </h4>
Smartcomply offers premium open banking services that enhance customer journeys while simplifying your processes as a service provider. We achieve this through:
High-performance, developer-friendly API systems that make app integration easy.
<p>An improved identity management system, with unique codes for every customer, ensuring easy identification.
Highly encrypted financial data on Smartcomply that is inaccessible to unauthorized third parties. Smartcomply adheres strictly to the Open Banking Regulatory Framework in Nigeria and other parts of Africa, keeping your organization legally compliant.</p>

<h4>Additional Benefits:</h4>
Full access to customers' financial data and biodata without writing any code.
1,000 free API call credits (limited).
24/7 customer service with a high "quick response" rate. Our products work swiftly.
