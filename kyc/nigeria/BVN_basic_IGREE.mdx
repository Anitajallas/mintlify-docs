## Verify your customers' identities using their Bank Verification Number (BVN) Basic IGREE

Adhere's advanced BVN verification endpoint offers enhanced KYC (Know Your Customer) capabilities by validating a customer's Bank Verification Number (BVN) with added support for date of birth verification. This ensures precise and reliable identification, helping businesses streamline user onboarding and prevent fraud.

The endpoint provides access to detailed personal information, including the customer's name, phone number, date of birth, National Identification Number (NIN), local government area of origin, state of origin, and customer image, among other key details. It also facilitates the auto-completion of account data, making it easier to identify users associated with specific financial institutions while improving overall security and operational efficiency.


#### Request Endpoint

`POST` `{{url}}/onboarding/nigeria_kyc/bvn_basic_igree` 


#### Sample Payload

```json
{
    "bvn": "22220000000", 
    "date_of_birth": "2002-04-01" # YYYY-MM-DD
}
```
