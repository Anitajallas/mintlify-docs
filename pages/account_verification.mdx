# Top-Notch Authentication Procedures

## Faster Onboarding & KYC Process

Make onboarding fast and effortless. Never ask for paper documents again. Auth retrieves account information instantly when users connect their account using login credentials.

## Reduce Fraudulent Sign-Ups

Automatically confirm users' identities with what's on file at the bank: their name, phone number, BVN, address, and email.

## Reduce Drop-Off

By making it easier—and quicker—for customers to sign up and start using your service, you increase the chances of them successfully completing the process.

## Seamless User Experience

Instantly check whether users have the funds to make a successful payment and increase your account funding rate with a seamless account and balance check.
