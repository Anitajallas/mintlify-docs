# Lend Confidently

## Verify Borrowers’ Assets Digitally

Provides a consolidated report with account balances, uncategorized and categorized transactions, and account identity information.

## Check Balances in Real-Time

Instantly checks whether users have the funds to make a payment or transfer.

## Instantly Authenticate Accounts

Pulls users' account and routing information, which can be used to enable seamless payments and debit withdrawals.

## Verify Borrowers' Identities

Confirms users' identities with what's on file at the bank—namely, their name, phone number, address, and email—to reduce fraud.
