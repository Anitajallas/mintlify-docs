# Getting Started with Smartcomply

To begin using Smartcomply, follow these steps:

## Complete Your Business KYC

Visit Smartcomply's KYC Portal to complete your business Know Your Customer (KYC) process. This step is essential to verify your business and ensure compliance with regulatory standards.

## Create Your Secret Key

After successfully completing your KYC, you can generate your secret key. This key will be used for authentication and secure access to Smartcomply's API services.

You're now ready to start integrating Smartcomply's financial infrastructure into your application!
