## Generate full credit report data with First Central

Adhere's Credit History endpoint furnishes users with a detailed record of their credit-related activities over time. It captures comprehensive data, including credit accounts, payment timelines, credit utilization, account closures, and instances of delinquency. By utilizing this endpoint, users gain valuable insights into their past credit behavior, allowing them to assess their creditworthiness, track changes, and understand factors influencing their credit standing. This endpoint serves as a valuable tool for users to monitor and manage their credit history effectively.

### Request Endpoint

`POST:` `{{BASE_URL}}/api/onboarding/individual/first_central_full/`

- **Media-Type:** `application/json`

### Status Codes
- **200 OK:** The request was successful, and the business' credit information is returned successfully.
- **400 Bad Request:** The request could not be completed due to invalid or missing information.

#### Sample Payload
```json
{
    "bvn": "22244545518"
}
```


200 OK Response
```json
{
    "status": "success",
    "data": {
        "_id": "64f600608aaf9386c646de32",
        "bvn": "22244545518",
        "name": "JOHN DOE",
        "score": {}
    },
    "message": "First Central check successful"
}
```


400 Bad Request Response
```json
{
    "status": "failed",
    "data": [],
    "message": "Sorry, your check cannot be processed at the moment. Please try again in a few minutes"
}
```
